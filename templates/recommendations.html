Limitations of the Recommendation System

  • Genre Complexity: The dataset's extensive genre variations complicate precise game comparisons.
  • Missing Data: Incomplete Metacritic scores can affect the accuracy of rating-based comparisons.
  • Computational Constraints: Limited computing resources may restrict the model's complexity.
  • Data Dependency: Recommendation quality is tied to the accuracy of the underlying game data.
  • Cold Start Problem: Accuracy may be reduced for new or less popular games with limited data.
